# Marketing Analytics & Customer Sentiment Analysis

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing marketing performance and customer feedback for **ShopEasy**, an online retail company experiencing declining customer engagement and conversion rates despite increased marketing investments.

The objective was to identify the root causes behind reduced marketing effectiveness, evaluate customer sentiment, and provide data-driven recommendations to improve engagement, conversions, and customer satisfaction.

The project combines marketing analytics, sentiment analysis, business intelligence, and data visualization to transform raw data into actionable business insights.

---

## Business Problem

ShopEasy faced several challenges:

* Declining customer engagement across marketing channels
* Reduced website conversion rates
* Increasing marketing expenses with lower ROI
* Need for deeper understanding of customer feedback and sentiment

The company required a comprehensive analysis of marketing performance metrics and customer reviews to support strategic decision-making.

## Key Findings

### Conversion Performance

* Conversion rates fluctuated throughout the year.
* January achieved the highest conversion rate at 18.5%.
* May recorded the lowest conversion rate at 4.3%.
* Products such as Ski Boots, Kayaks, and Baseball Gloves demonstrated strong conversion performance.

### Customer Engagement

* Audience views peaked during February and July.
* Engagement declined significantly during the second half of the year.
* Blog content generated the highest number of views.
* Clicks and likes remained relatively low compared to impressions.

### Customer Feedback

* Customer ratings remained relatively stable around 3.7/5.
* Most reviews were rated 4 or 5 stars.
* Positive sentiment dominated customer feedback.
* Mixed and negative reviews revealed opportunities for service and product improvements.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Improve Conversion Rates

* Focus marketing budgets on high-converting product categories.
* Launch targeted seasonal campaigns during peak-performing months.
* Optimize landing pages and conversion funnels.

### Increase Customer Engagement

* Introduce interactive content formats.
* Improve social media calls-to-action.
* Leverage user-generated content and video marketing.
* Re-engage audiences during lower-performing months.

### Enhance Customer Satisfaction

* Monitor and analyze negative reviews regularly.
* Address recurring customer concerns proactively.
* Implement customer feedback loops.
* Improve product experiences to achieve an average rating above 4.0.
